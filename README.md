🛒 BlinkIT Grocery Sales Analytics Dashboard

📌 Description

This project presents an interactive BlinkIT Grocery Sales Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze sales performance, product trends, outlet performance, and customer preferences.

The dashboard transforms raw grocery sales data into meaningful business insights, helping stakeholders monitor key metrics, identify growth opportunities, and make data-driven decisions.

🚨 Business Problem

Retail grocery businesses often struggle with:

Understanding which products drive the highest sales
Comparing performance across different outlet locations
Identifying customer preferences and buying patterns
Monitoring outlet performance and profitability
Evaluating the impact of outlet size and type on revenue

Without proper analytics, businesses may face inventory inefficiencies, reduced profitability, and missed growth opportunities.

🎯 Project Objectives
Analyze overall sales performance using key business KPIs
Identify top-performing product categories
Evaluate sales distribution across outlet types and locations
Understand the impact of outlet size on revenue generation
Analyze customer ratings and product preferences
Provide actionable recommendations for business growth

Average customer satisfaction rating.

📈 Average Sales

Average revenue generated per transaction/item.

🧮 Key DAX Measures
🔹 Total Sales
Total Sales = SUM('BlinkIT Data'[Sales])
🔹 Number of Items
Number of Items = COUNT('BlinkIT Data'[Item Identifier])
🔹 Average Sales
Average Sales = AVERAGE('BlinkIT Data'[Sales])
🔹 Average Rating
Average Rating = AVERAGE('BlinkIT Data'[Rating])
